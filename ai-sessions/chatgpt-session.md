## Notes

- The discussion helped me review the intended dashboard requirements: providing a portfolio overview, filtering properties by onboarding status, and displaying a detailed onboarding checklist for individual properties.
- During the initial planning process, I used AI assistance to challenge my understanding of the requirements and identify areas where my interpretation of the task could have led me in the wrong direction.
- After reviewing the feedback and gaining a clearer understanding of the expected outcome, I decided to utilise agentic AI tools to help speed up the development process while still maintaining responsibility for the implementation decisions and code quality after agentic AI made the changes.
- The AI assistance was used to accelerate tasks such as structuring components, connecting existing functionality, implementing dashboard features, and reviewing potential improvements rather than replacing my own understanding of React, TypeScript, and Next.js.
- I reviewed and adjusted the generated suggestions to ensure the final implementation matched the requirements and remained consistent with the existing project structure.
- This session is included to document how AI tools were incorporated into my workflow, the reasoning behind implementation choices, and how I maintained human oversight throughout development.
